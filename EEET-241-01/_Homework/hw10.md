    $$
        Z_1=\sqrt{R_1^2 +x^2}=\sqrt{0.7^2 +5^2}\\
    Z_1=5.048\Omega\\
        \alpha=tan^{-1}({X\over R_1})=tan^{-1}({5\Omega\over 0.7\Omega})\\
    \alpha=82.03^\circ
        $$
    
    
    2. The speed when the breakdown torque is reached
        $$
        n_b=n_s(1-({R_2\over Z_1}))=900(1-({0.5\Omega\over 5.048\Omega}))\\

        $$

        $$
